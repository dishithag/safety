## Zero Trust Audit Narrative

### Executive Summary

This environment shows partial alignment with the target Zero Trust posture, but
several controls still need attention. The strongest signals are around basic
reporting coverage and available audit evidence. The largest gaps appear in
control consistency and follow-through on higher-risk findings.

### Key Findings

1. Multiple controls are present, but not all are consistently enforced across
   the assessed scope.
2. The audit data suggests uneven maturity between covered areas, which can
   create operational blind spots.
3. Higher-priority remediation items should be addressed before expanding to
   lower-impact improvements.

### Recommended Next Steps

1. Review the highest-risk failed or missing controls first and confirm owners.
2. Validate that the same controls are applied consistently across all relevant
   assets and identities.
3. Re-run the reporting flow after remediation to confirm the posture improves.
